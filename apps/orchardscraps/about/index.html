About Orchard's Craps

An overview of Orchard's Craps.

Orchard’s Craps is the first in the Orchard's Casino series.

Orchard's Craps is a classic casino dice game where players place bets on the outcome of the roll. They say that craps is based on an Old English game named Hazards which dates back to the Crusades. So craps is steeped in tradition and played the world over.

To begin a player places a bet on the PASS or DON'T PASS line and becomes the Shooter. Orchard's Craps highlights all playable areas on the table so you can see where you can place additional bets. Once the initial bet is placed the Shooter can either tap the dice or shake the iPhone/iPod touch to roll. The first roll is called the Come Out Roll and is used to set the Point.

As you roll the dice bounce around the table much like at a casino, and when they stop a Stickman calls the roll (yes, an actual person calls the rolls). Bets are paid out or collected and if the shooter doesn't "crap out" he/she continues to roll.

Orchard's Craps is a fun, fast-paced game of skill and chance. There are many more things to learn about craps, and for this we have included Wikipedia's entry on craps in Orchard's Craps so that you can learn as you play. We will also be releasing links to tutorials and videos in upcoming releases but you should download Orchard's Craps and start playing now.
