Dinosaurs?

A learning game about dinosuars.

Dinosaurs? is a game of questions and answers. Think of a dinosaur and Dinosaurs? tries to guess it.

Answer simple yes/no questions like "Does it live in water?" or "Does it have horns?" When Dinosaurs? thinks it knows the answer it will guess the dinosaur: "Is it a triceratops?"

When Dinosaurs? makes a wrong guess you can teach it the new dinosaur and add a new question to ask.

Dinosaurs? is great game for anyone age 8 and up who loves geography.
